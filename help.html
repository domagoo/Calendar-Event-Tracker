  1. Introduction to Southern Charm Events Calendar

    Welcome to the Southern Charm Events Calendar, or SCEC. This system allows you to schedule events, save event information such as the amount due and vendor contacts, print out the calendar and events, and receive notification reminders for due dates. This document will guide you through the various different features and tools available to schedule events.

  2. Login

    When accessing SCEC, you will first be taken to the Login Page. Here you will input your SCEC email and password. The password will be provided by the development team.

    An email and password must be entered at the Login page. Error messages are displayed when text is not entered. An error message is also displayed for invalid logins.

    1. Reset Password

      If you forget the password to your account, you can reset it by clicking Reset Password on the Login Page. You will be taken to the Reset Password page where you enter the email address associated with the account. An email will be sent to the corresponding email address with instructions.

    3. Homepage

      After logging into SCEC using your credentials, you will be taken to the SCEC homepage, which will show a calendar of the current week, month, or year. There are several options on homepage.

      Use the left and right arrows to navigate between the weeks, months, or years. The Today button will take you back to the current week, month, or year.

    1. Weekly View

      The Weekly button will display the current week along with events scheduled and due dates.

    2. Monthly View

      The Monthly button will display the current month. Click the Events button to show all events scheduled for that month. Click the Due Dates button to show all due dates for that month.

    3. Yearly View

      The Yearly button will show a yearly calendar with the number of events scheduled for each month. Click on a month to view that month in the Monthly view.

  4. Create, Edit, and Delete Events

    In this example, we will walk through the create, edit, and delete pages of SCEC.

    1. Create New Events

      To schedule a new event, navigate to the date where the event will take place in either the Weekly or Monthly view and click on the date.

      On the right a new sidebar will temporarily replace the notifications, letting you enter the event name, date, time, type of event, client name, vendor information (name, type, contact), and the total charge.

      After entering all information, click Save to save it to the calendar. Click Cancel if you do not want it to be saved to the calendar.

    2. Editing Events

      To view or edit an event, click on the event on either the Weekly or Monthly view. On the right, the event details will display. Click on any of the textboxes and edit the text, then click the Edit button to save the changes.

    3. Deleting Events

      To delete an event, click on the event on either the Weekly or Monthly view. On the right, the event details will display. Click the Delete button on the bottom right, then click the OK button on the prompt to delete the event. This action cannot be undone.

  5. Notifications and Due Dates

    1. Notifications

      By default, the right sidebar will display upcoming due dates for events in the calendar. The software will output a notification reminder for remaining balance due, damage deposit, and police officer fee 60 days prior to the event. It will output a notification reminder for the vendors' agreement and vendors' insurance 30 days prior to the event. It will output a notification reminder for the floor plan and timeline 14 days prior to the event. Once a task has been marked as complete on the Event Details screen, its notification will be dismissed.

      The software will output a notification on the 15th of every month to remind the user to email the police office and the cleaning staff. Clicking on one of these notifications will dismiss it until the next month. All notifications for an event, whether marked as complete or not, will disappear from the sidebar after the event date has passed.

    2. Due Dates

      Clicking the Due Dates button on the Monthly View will display the upcoming due dates for events. The Due Dates on the Weekly View show more details, including counts for every type of task that is due.

  6. Miscellaneous

    1. Backup/Restore

      Click the More… button near the upper right corner of the calendar, then click Backup to create a backup of all events. To restore a backup, click the Restore… button, then select a backup to restore.

      Up to 20 backups can be stored at one time, with the oldest ones being deleted first. To avoid losing events that were created after the backup that is being restored, it is recommended to only restore backups in the case of significant data loss. Note that older events can still be viewed in the calendar without using the backup feature.

    2. Printing

      Navigate to the view you would like to print (Weekly, Monthly, or Yearly). Click the More… button near the upper right corner of the calendar, then click the Print button to bring up printing options.

      To print out specific event details, navigate to the event on the calendar and click on it. Click on the print button on the bottom of the Event Details pane to bring up printing options.

    3. Logout

      When you are finished using SCEC, use the Log Out button to sign out of the system. You will be directed back to the Login page.
